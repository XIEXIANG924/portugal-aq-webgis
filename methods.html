Methods & Data

Data sources, processing pipeline, and technical infrastructure

Data Sources

CAMS Reanalysis

Copernicus Atmosphere Monitoring Service. PM₂.₅, NO₂, PM₁₀ monthly aggregated raster data for 2021 and 2023. December 2023 computed from raw hourly NetCDF (744 bands) using GDAL band averaging. Resolution: 0.1°.

Copernicus Land Cover

ESRI 10m Annual Land Cover (2017–2024) from ArcGIS Living Atlas. Classes extracted: Trees (2), Crops (5), Built Area (7). Binary mask differencing for 2021→2023 change detection.

WorldPop 2023

Population Counts at 100m (R2025A v1) for Portugal. Resampled to 0.1° with Sum aggregation to preserve total population for exposure analysis.

GAUL Boundaries

Portugal administrative boundary (EPSG:4326) from Global Administrative Unit Layers. Reprojected to EPSG:3035 for area-based Bonus calculations.

Processing Pipeline

Pollutant Maps — Dec 2023

Raw hourly NetCDF (744 bands) processed with GDAL to compute monthly mean concentrations at 0.1°. Rasterized and clipped to Portugal.

Annual Averages — 2023

Twelve monthly rasters clipped and aggregated using r.series (average). Published as WMS.

Concentration Classification

Annual averages reclassified into 5 EU health guideline classes. Green (good) through purple (hazardous). Published as WMS with SLD styling.

AMAC — Aggregation Change

Raster Calculator: 2023 − 2021. Six-class discrete symbology with QML export. Core WebGIS layer.

Land Cover Change

Binary mask differencing at 10m → Gain/Loss/Stable maps. EPSG:3035 area statistics for Bonus evaluation.

Zonal Statistics

AMAC values extracted per LC change category at pixel level. Mean/min/max for each pollutant–land cover pair.

Bivariate Analysis

Population density (3 classes) × Pollution concentration (3 classes) → 3×3 bivariate classification per admin unit.

Population Exposure

Resampled population masked by concentration classes. Doughnut charts showing proportion per air quality level.

Technology Stack

QGIS 3.40 + GDAL

Primary GIS processing: GRASS r.series, GDAL Warp, Raster Calculator, Reclassify by Table.

GeoServer (OGC WMS)

Polimi Online GeoServer publishing 10 layers including AMAC, annual averages, and concentration maps.

OpenLayers 10

Interactive WebGIS with dual basemaps, Scale Line, Full Screen, Mouse Position, and grouped layer switching.

Chart.js 4

AMAC bar charts and population exposure doughnut charts. Responsive, dark-text-on-light design.

GitHub Pages

Static site hosting. No backend required. All GIS layers served via GeoServer WMS.
