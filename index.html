About This Project

Understanding the spatial relationship between land transformation and atmospheric quality in Portugal

Objective

Quantify NO₂, PM₂.₅, and PM₁₀ concentration trends across Portugal (2021–2023) and correlate changes with land cover transitions using Copernicus data.

Approach

Multi-temporal raster analysis with zonal statistics, bivariate classification of pollution vs. population exposure, and OGC-compliant interactive WebGIS.

Outcome

Comprehensive air quality maps, land cover change detection, population exposure assessment, and a public-facing WebGIS on GeoServer + OpenLayers.
